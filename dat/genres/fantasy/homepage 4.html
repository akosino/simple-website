The Hobbit

The Hobbit by J.R.R. Tolkien is a fantasy classic that follows Bilbo Baggins on an adventure through Middle-earth, where he encounters trolls, goblins, and the dragon Smaug guarding a treasure.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone by J.K. Rowling is the start of an epic fantasy series about a young boy who discovers he is a wizard and begins his journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

The Name of the Wind

The Name of the Wind by Patrick Rothfuss follows the story of Kvothe, a gifted young man who recounts his journey through a world filled with magic, music, and intrigue.
