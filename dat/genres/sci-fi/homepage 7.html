Dune

Dune by Frank Herbert is a science fiction epic set on the desert planet of Arrakis, where noble families vie for control of a valuable resource while navigating politics, religion, and prophecy.

Ender's Game

Ender's Game by Orson Scott Card follows young Ender Wiggin, who is trained at a military academy to become the ultimate commander in humanity’s battle against an alien species.

Neuromancer

Neuromancer by William Gibson is a cyberpunk classic that explores artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and hacking as a washed-up computer hacker embarks on a mission to hack into a powerful AI system.
